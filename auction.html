What are auction lobbies ?
Auction lobbies are Daily Auctions that offer CSE tokens for ETH based on the CSE and ETH pool at the end of the day.
How much CSE do lobbies pay ?
Lobbies CSE pool will start from 5 million CSE per day and goes until 1 million over the next 365 days but never goes lower than 1 million tokens per day.
Buying tokens from exchnage or participating in auction ?
Auction lobbies are an other way to buy CSE tokens that might be more profitable that purchasing on exchanges. Plus the ETH spent is rewarded back to Stakers.
Where does all the ETH go ?
Dividends, well at least 90% of them !! The other 10% reserve for T2X platform.
When do lobbies end ?
Lobbies are daily, Every day at 00:00 UTC
