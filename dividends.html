ETHEREUM Dividends

One of the benefits of Staking CSE is ETHEREUM dividends. At the end of each day a ETHEREUM dividends pool will be calculated and allocated to all the open stakes based on their stake amount. The ETHEREUM dividends pool comes from the total daily entry of auction lobby. The only way to receive ETHEREUM Dividends is having open stakes
