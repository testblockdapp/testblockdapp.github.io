Page FaQ

What is staking?
You can stake your CSE token for a fixed number of days to earn interest on them. Make sure to stake more than 5 days to be eligible to get bonus days reward.
At the end of every day a daily stake pool of CSE tokens will be calculated and it will be shared and allocate to all the open stakes between all the open stakes based on their staked tokens amount which will be available for user to withdraw when the stake ends.
How is stake pool calculated ?
[CSE supply * 50000 / 5.42% inflation per year ], this daily pool will be distributed between all the Stakers as their stake interest.
Bonus for Staking ?
For starting a stake you get bonus reward for your stake which is based on the amount and time that you are staking
What is a reward day ?
Every stake will get a bonus day every 5 days that it has been active, stakes get 2X interest on bonus days. Example: creating a stake at day 4 for 15 days gives this stake 3 bonus at days 10,15, and day 20 etc.
What if i end my stake early ?
You are subject up to 100% penatly based upon your stake terms.
Do i get ETH dividends for staking ?
Yes! One of the benefits of Staking CSE is ETH dividends. At the end of each day a ETH dividends pool will be calculated and allocated to all the open stakes based on their stake amount. The dividends pool comes from the daily auction lobby total entry amount.
How is dividends pool calculated ?
90% of all ETH that enters the daily Auction Lobby is pooled and distributed back out to Stakers.
When will I receive my ETH dividends ?
After you exit your stakes you will receive your ETH dividends
